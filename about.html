About me

Hi, my name is Dorant, and I'm a passionate landscape photographer. I grew up surrounded by nature, and I've always been fascinated by the beauty and diversity of our planet's landscapes. My photography journey started when I received my first camera as a gift from my parents, and I quickly fell in love with capturing the world around me. Over the years, I've honed my skills and developed my own unique style, inspired by Gothic and Victorian era literature such as Bram Stoker's "Dracula". I'm constantly on the lookout for new places to explore and capture through my lens. Whether it's a hidden gem in my own backyard or a remote wilderness in a far-off land, I'm always seeking to capture the essence and beauty of each location. My goal as a photographer is to share my love of the natural world and inspire others to appreciate and protect our planet's landscapes. I hope my photos transport you to some of the most stunning places on earth and help you experience the wonder of nature in a new way.
